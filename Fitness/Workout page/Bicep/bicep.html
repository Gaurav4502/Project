Bicep Workout Guide

1. Barbell Curls

Builds overall bicep mass and strength.

2. Dumbbell Hammer Curls

Targets the brachialis and forearms.

3. Concentration Curls

Isolates the biceps for peak contraction.

4. Preacher Curls

Focuses on the lower bicep for better definition.

5. Incline Dumbbell Curls

Stretches and targets the long head of the biceps.

6. Cable Curls

Provides constant tension on the biceps.
